  helm upgrade ro-op-crds oci://ghcr.io/tjorri/charts/runtime-overrides-operator-crds \

    --version <new-version> \
    --set crds.install=false

The two charts are versioned in lockstep, so the chart versions and the
operator image tag (appVersion) all move together.
